# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Dataset
Sample Superstore Sales Dataset

## Tool Used
Power BI

## Visualizations Created
- Total Sales by Category
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Category
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Customer Segment

## Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest sales
- West region leads in revenue
- Furniture shows lower profitability
- Consumer segment contributes most sales
- Sales peak during year-end months
